# Lead Scoring v0.1

## Goal
Prioritize companies most likely to purchase the selected product.

## Score rules

### Product and industry fit: 0–30
- Exact target-industry match: 30
- Related industry: 15
- Weak or unclear match: 0

### Company quality: 0–20
- Clear company website and business description: 10
- Established brand, distributor, retailer, or event/display company: 10

### Contactability: 0–20
- Public business email: 10
- Named buyer, purchasing manager, owner, sourcing manager, or marketing manager: 10

### Purchase signal: 0–20
- Uses similar displays or custom printed products: 10
- Import history, supplier inquiry, RFQ, or active product range: 10

### Market fit: 0–10
- Target country: 10
- Other country: 0

## Grade mapping
- A: 70–100 — contact quickly
- B: 40–69 — add to outreach queue
- C: 0–39 — retain for long-term nurturing

## Default follow-up policy
- A: first contact within 1 business day
- B: first contact within 7 days
- C: no immediate outreach; keep in database
